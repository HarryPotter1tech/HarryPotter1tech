### 🧑 About Me

Embedded systems & robotics developer from **Nanjing, China**. Specializing in STM32/HPM real-time control, RoboMaster competition systems, SDR radar, solid-state LiDAR (WS_30PCD / Livox / Odin), ROS2, and SLAM.

Currently focused on **multi-sensor fusion** (LiDAR + camera + radar) for autonomous systems and RoboMaster 2026 preparation. Also exploring TTS, Blender animation, and Rust GUI development.
